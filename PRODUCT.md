# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Tech recruiters and hiring managers scanning quickly for signal. They have limited time and are looking for clear evidence of skill, relevant experience, and fit. Secondary audience: potential collaborators and engineering teams doing a background check before reaching out.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio for Luis-Angel Moreno, a CS student at Florida International University with Salesforce internship experience. The site exists to convert recruiter attention into interviews and opportunities. Success means a recruiter lands, finds exactly what they need without effort, and leaves with confidence in Luis as a candidate.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, ambitious, technical. Not flashy — precise. Confidence through clarity, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Over-complicated portfolios: busy layouts, excessive animations, flashy effects that bury the actual content
- Portfolios where you have to hunt for the important information (experience, skills, projects)
- Generic "developer template" aesthetics: dark mode with neon accents, glowing cards, matrix-style effects
- Overly casual or warm-and-fuzzy designs that undercut technical seriousness

## Design Principles

1. **Signal competence, not decoration** — every visual choice should reinforce skill and judgment, not distract from it. Good taste is itself evidence.
2. **Recruiter-first clarity** — the most important information (experience, skills, projects) is always immediately visible and scannable. No hunting.
3. **Earned weight** — visual emphasis is reserved for what matters most. Don't shout everything.
4. **Show, don't explain** — let work and experience carry the argument. Minimal prose, maximum evidence.
5. **Sharp restraint** — ambitious doesn't mean loud. Confidence comes from precision, not volume.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Smooth scroll behavior maintained. Reduced motion should be respected for entrance animations.
